G Code Reference

Word Addresses:
X## = X position Y## = Y position Z## = Z position
I## = X arc center J## = Y arc center K## = Z arc center
F## = feedrate P## = dwell time in 10000th sec

About Us

MillRight CNC is making desktop CNC affordable. Mill wood, plastics, PCBs, and even aluminum.

A low cost, reliable CNC machine that lets you do real work has long been sought after by CNC enthusiasts. MillRight CNC decided to make that a reality. We set out to design machines that offered an outstanding value for the money and we have delivered! Customers all over the world are making amazing projects with their MillRight CNC machine and we're proud to have made that possible.

Our continued commitment is to maintain the best performance to price ratio of any CNC kit on the market. We are an American company run in the USA. All employees are in the United States, including support.

MillRight CNC M3

Starting at $299

Standard Features:

  • Laser cut, anodized aluminum bearing plates
  • V slot rails on X & Y axes with acetal V wheels
  • Linear rods on Z Axis with linear bearing blocks
  • CNC routed MDF frame
  • High torque NEMA 17 stepper motors
  • Steel motor mounts for the X & Y
  • 9mm fiberglass reinforced GT2 timing belts
  • Mechanically advantaged belt driven Z Axis for fast lifts
  • Space efficient moving bed design
  • Pronged tee nuts in the bed for easy work holding
  • Generic Uno, USB controller board
  • Grbl compatible stepper driver shield and DRV8825 drivers
  • 24V Volt, 120 Watt power supply

Available features (additional cost):

  • Black epoxy painting that is resistant to cutting oils and moisture.
  • Homing switch kits that include 3 snap switches, switch brackets, hardware, and wiring. Wire splicing and connector crimping required with this upgrade.
  • Rigidity kit including two extra V wheels for the X axis plate and three #10 threaded rods for frame stiffening.
  • Stage 2 rigidity kit includes the above plus brushed stainless steel bearing plates instead of anodized aluminum plates. The stainless plates are about THREE TIMES STIFFER than the aluminum plates.

NOT Included:

Router, router mount, work clamps, or end mills. See our other products to add a router and router mount or spindle. Contact us at millrightcnc@gmail.com if you would like us to source these items for you. Although they are not listed, we can do so for a charge.

Software is not included but everything you need is available for FREE.

Capabilities:

  • Mills wood, plastics, printed circuit boards, and can even make light cuts in aluminum

Working Area:

  • Over 10.25''×10.25''×2'' (260mm × 260mm × 50mm)

Starting at $699

The Carve King is the new standard in affordable CNC machines. Its thoughtful design elements allow it to outperform the competion at a lower price.

Standard Features:

  • Laser cut, anodized 1/4" 6061 aluminum bearing plates
  • Trapezoidal lead screws with anti-backlash nuts on every axis
  • V Slot rails with rugged polycarbonate V wheels and double shielded bearings
  • CNC routed, double-refined 3/4" MDF frame
  • High torque NEMA 17 stepper motors with dual drive on the Y axis
  • ST-Track extrusions in bed for superior clamping options
  • Drag chains for wire management
  • Uno USB controller board preloaded with Grbl control firmware
  • Stepper driver shield and DRV8825 drivers
  • 24V Volt, 120 Watt power supply
  • Laser cut acrylic electronics enclosure with cooling fan
  • Basic work clamps included

  • Homing switch kits that include 3 snap switches, switch brackets, hardware, and wiring. Wire splicing and connector crimping required with this upgrade.

NOT Included:

Router, router mount, vise, end mills. See our other products to add a router and router mount or spindle. Vise available at large tool retailers.

Software is not included but everything you need is available for FREE.
